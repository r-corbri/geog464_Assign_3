Sonoma County Fire Wildland Fire Risk Map

Climate change is leading to more frequent severe weather events across the globe, and California is an especially sensitive area in North America when it comes to severe weather. Long droughts that are exacerbated by climate change along with steep topography and a dispersed population means the state is highly prone to frequent damaging wildfires. This map was made to provide a simple visual illustration of this risk to those living in Sonoma county California. The goal of this risk mapping is to help residents understand the level of fire risk that their communities are under, something that is not always apparent to those living high risk areas. This map provides awareness to those living in high risk areas, as well as where future development should be avoided to limit building into high risk areas.
