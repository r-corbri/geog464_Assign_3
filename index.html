Sonoma County Fire Wildland Fire Risk Map

This is a map of a map of the fire risk in risk in Sonoma county. This map is intended to help residents and land owners understand what the severity of fire risk thier property and communites are prone to given the surronding landscape.

How this map is intended to be used

This map includes a fire risk layer, historic fire perimiters (1950 - 2024), as well as fire stations in Sonoma county.

Layers can be turned on and off in the top right of the page.

Clicking on a specfic point will tell you the fire risk in that area.

Addresses can be searched by clicking on the magnifying glass icon in the top right hand of the map.

Base map options can also be changed in the top right of the page.
